Welcome

Welcome to Lightspeed Restaurant!

There are two main parts of Lightspeed Restaurant, the Manager and the Point of Sale.

Most of the setup is done through the Manager, which can be accessed from both an iPad and a web browser on a regular computer. We recommend using a regular computer for ease of use while setting up.

The POS (Point Of Sale) app is used for taking orders, processing payments, and day-to-day tasks such as opening and closing the cash register counts.

IMPORTANT SETTINGS FOR ALL USERS - PLEASE READ

Taxes
-First, check to see if Tax Inclusive pricing is turned On or Off. This feature is typically turned off in North America:

1. From the Manager click on Settings in the top-right corner of the screen.
2. Scroll down to App Settings and check (to turn on) or uncheck (to turn off) the option Include VAT.
3. Press Save at the bottom of the window.

-Make sure your taxes are correctly applied to your products. The easiest way to set a Tax Rate for a group of products is to set it by Category

-There is the option to set different tax rates for regular orders, delivery, and take-out orders seperately. Make sure these are set in order to properly calculate and charge taxes.
